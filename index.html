How We Met

LOVE AT FIRST SIGHT...WELL AT LEAST FOR HIM

I want to take you back to the day our story began, June 15, 2009, my first day of class during the summer before my freshman year at Saints. When we met, it was like 33 meeting 36 for the first time.

That morning, as I drearily walked across the campus towards my class, I began to see what life at Saints would look like. I was only there because my parents had decided for me to “get ahead” in school and urged me to take this math class. Part of my summer vacation was now devoted to school (ugh), but at least I could get an early taste of this "Saints Experience" I’d heard so much about.

I opened a glass door and I walked into a chilly, half-full Algebra I classroom. I took a seat in the back-left like the cool kid I knew I was. Two seconds later, I heard a nervous and excited voice say “Oye, eres Mexicano?” I looked over.

Who knew I was into redheads?

I don’t know what won me over first. Was it the soft (yet manly) voice with a bit of a voice crack? Was it the muscles of a cocky 14 year-old tennis player? At that moment, I just knew that I had a new goal in mind involving this redhead. Over the next summer weeks, I did my best to charm him.

We talked.

We laughed.

We shared our dreams.

We got yelled at by the teacher and cursed out by some weird short kid.

Suffice to say that we'd followed the right Order of Operations and continued to take our relationship to the next level.

Now 12 years strong with him, I still call him a bitch.

-The Groom
